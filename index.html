ABOUT US

We're an Adelaide based independent retailer of boutique car care products and an authorised reseller of Wizards Products, specialty appearance products manufactured in the United States.

Located within the Thunderbird Slot Racing raceway & shop, we stock an extensive range of Wizards products covering all aspects of car and motorcycle interior and exterior care.

We import our stock in bulk directly from the manufacturer which allows us offer the best available prices in South Australia across our range.

WIZARDS PRODUCTS

Wizards have been manufacturing a complete professional line of automotive and motorcycle appearance products in the United States since 1986.

Based on years of manufacturing experience as well as extensive customer feedback, Wizards products are of the highest performance and enjoy an excellent reputation amonst industry and the enthusiast community.
